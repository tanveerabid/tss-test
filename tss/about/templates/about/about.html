Tight security Services (Pvt) Ltd, having NOC from Ministry of Interior is registered under Companies Ordinance 1984(Companies act 2017) with SECP since 2006.

Right after its incorporation, the company also obtained NOC from the provincial authorities of Punjab under the PPSC (regulations & control Ordinance 2002).

The company achieved another milestone in 2015, when NOC of Federal Administration of Islamabad ICT is issued to the company. Since then the company is fully operational in both Punjab and ICT Islamabad.
